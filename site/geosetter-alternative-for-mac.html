Mac photo geotagging

A GeoSetter alternative for Mac reference-photo workflows

GeoSetter is a respected Windows geotagging tool. GeoTagCopy gives Mac users a focused path for one specific job: copying GPS coordinates from phone photos to camera photos by timestamp.

What GeoTagCopy handles

Pick a folder of geotagged phone photos and a folder of untagged camera photos. GeoTagCopy groups likely matches by location so you can inspect them before ExifTool writes GPS metadata.

Why photographers search for this

Many cameras still ship without built-in GPS, and phone-linked camera apps can fail or be forgotten during a shoot. A local post-shoot utility gives you a repair workflow when the location data already exists in nearby iPhone or Android photos.

Useful for

  • GeoSetter alternative for Mac
  • Mac app to copy GPS from phone photos
  • Geotag camera photos after the shoot
  • Use iPhone photos as GPS references for camera photos
