Mac photo geotagging

A GeoSetter alternative for Mac reference photos

GeoSetter is a Windows tool. GeoTagCopy gives Mac users a way to copy GPS from phone photos to camera photos by timestamp, then check the matches before writing anything.

What GeoTagCopy handles

Pick a folder of geotagged phone photos and a folder of untagged camera photos. GeoTagCopy groups likely matches by location so you can check them before ExifTool writes GPS metadata.

Why photographers search for this

Many cameras still ship without built-in GPS, and camera companion apps can fail or be forgotten during a shoot. GeoTagCopy is useful when the location data already exists in nearby iPhone or Android photos and you are fixing the camera files later.

What this page is for

If you searched for a GeoSetter alternative for Mac, or for a Mac app to copy GPS from phone photos after a shoot, this is the use case GeoTagCopy covers.
