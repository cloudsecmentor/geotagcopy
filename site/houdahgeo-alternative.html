Reference-photo geotagging

A HoudahGeo alternative for one smaller job

HoudahGeo does a lot. GeoTagCopy stays with one task: copy GPS from phone reference photos to camera photos by timestamp, then let you check the matches before anything gets written.

GeoTagCopy makes sense when

  • You already have geotagged phone photos from the same shoot.
  • You do not want to create or import GPX tracklogs.
  • You want to check matches before writing metadata.
  • You prefer open source software you can inspect.

When HoudahGeo may fit better

Use a larger tool if you need map editing, reverse geocoding, video geotagging, or Apple Photos and Lightroom integration. GeoTagCopy is for repairing missing GPS on camera files after the shoot.

What GeoTagCopy is trying to be

It is not trying to clone a full geocoding suite. It is for the moment when the location data is already sitting in your phone photos and you just need to move it onto the camera files.
