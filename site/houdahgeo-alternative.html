Focused geotagging utility

A HoudahGeo alternative for simple reference-photo geotagging

HoudahGeo is a broad Mac geotagging suite. GeoTagCopy is narrower: it focuses on copying GPS coordinates from phone reference photos to camera photos by timestamp, with a local review step before writing.

Choose GeoTagCopy when

  • You already have geotagged phone photos from the same shoot.
  • You do not want to create or import GPX tracklogs.
  • You want a lightweight desktop workflow built around preview and apply.
  • You prefer open-source software you can inspect or run from source.

Choose a larger suite when

A broader tool may be a better fit if you need map-based manual editing, reverse geocoding, video geotagging, or deep Apple Photos and Lightroom integration. GeoTagCopy is intentionally aimed at the smaller, frequent annoyance of repairing missing GPS on camera files after a shoot.

Positioning

The goal is not to clone a full geocoding suite. GeoTagCopy tries to be the fast path for hybrid shooters who already have the location data in their phone photos and just need it copied safely to camera photos.
