# Privacy

GeoTagCopy is designed to run locally.

## Desktop App

The desktop app does not collect telemetry, upload photos, upload filenames, or
send EXIF metadata to a GeoTagCopy service. Photo scanning, matching, previewing,
and metadata writing happen on your computer.

The app may open external websites in your default browser when you choose links
such as project support, source code, or release downloads. Those sites have
their own privacy practices.

## Website

The project website is static. If analytics are added later, prefer
privacy-preserving aggregate analytics and do not collect photo metadata,
filenames, folder names, or location-derived data from the desktop app.

## Support Payments

Voluntary support payments should be handled by Stripe-hosted pages opened in
the browser. GeoTagCopy should not collect or process card details inside the
desktop app.
